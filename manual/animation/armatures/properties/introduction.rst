Motion Paths
============

.. admonition:: Reference
   :class: refbox

   :Mode:      All Modes
   :Panel:     :menuselection:`Armature --> Motion Paths`

In the :doc:`Motion Paths </animation/motion_paths>` panel you can enable visualization
of the motion path your skeleton leaves when animated.
