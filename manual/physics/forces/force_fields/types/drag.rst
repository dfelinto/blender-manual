****
Drag
****

.. reference::

   :Panel:     :menuselection:`Physics --> Force Fields`
   :Type:      Drag

A *Drag* force field resists particle motion by slowing it down.

=======

   Drag component proportional to velocity.
Quadratic
   Drag component proportional to the square of the velocity.
