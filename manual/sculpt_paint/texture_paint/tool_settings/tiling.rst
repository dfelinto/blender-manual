******
Tiling
******

.. reference::

   :Editor:    Image Editor
   :Mode:      Paint Mode
   :Menu:      :menuselection:`Sidebar --> Tools --> Tiling`

Wraps the stroke to the other side of the image as your brush moves off the opposite side of the canvas.
Very handy for making seamless textures.

X
   left/right
Y
   top/bottom
