
*****
Pinch
*****

.. admonition:: Reference
   :class: refbox

   :Mode:      Sculpt Mode
   :Tool:      :menuselection:`Toolbar --> Pinch`
   :Hotkey:    :kbd:`P`

Pulls vertices towards the center of the brush.
The inverse setting is *Magnify*, in which vertices are pushed away from the center of the brush.
